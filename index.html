About Us

OFW Connect is a support platform created to empower and protect Overseas Filipino Workers by providing accessible information, guidance, and community support.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support OFWs with legal guidance, mental health awareness, emergency assistance, and resources for life abroad.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every OFW feels safe, informed, and supported, wherever they are.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, and unity drive OFW Connect. We promote respect, empathy, and collaboration.

Our Services

We offer essential services to help OFWs navigate challenges abroad.

Legal Help

  • Employment contract guidance
  • Labor rights awareness
  • Workplace dispute advice
  • Referrals to legal agencies

Mental Health Support

  • Stress and anxiety management
  • Coping with homesickness
  • Self-care tips
  • Access to hotlines

Emergency Support

  • Embassy and consulate contacts
  • Emergency hotlines
  • Abuse and exploitation guidance
  • Urgent procedures
